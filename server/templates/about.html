Questions

Where does the data come from?
All the data was sourced from Wikipedia so do not use it as an authority.
How is a connection determined?
The connections are the context in which one politician appears on another person's page. For example, if Hillary Clinton is mentioned as Obama's Secretary of State on his page, then that is a bi-directional connection.
Why can't I find person X when searching?
The queries are case sensitive and fairly static. If you still are not able to find your desired person, ensure your query is exactly how it appears in Wikipedia's URL. For example, George H Bush's is https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Herbert_Walker_Bush, so one must eneter George Herbert Walker Bush. H W, george b, or bush will not work. If you are still unsuccessful, then either contact me or adjust your query.
Why doesn't my returned result make any sense?
The context taken from Wikipedia may not be complete sentences and during data gathering simple NLP was used to extract the context so it may not be optimal.
What can I do if there is misinformation or connection path isn't optimal?
If you're unsatisfied with the results or wish to see an aleration you can also contact me.
